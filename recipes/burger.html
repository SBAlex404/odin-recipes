Description

Recipe

Set out a large mixing bowl. Add in the ground beef, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire sauce, milk, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and pepper. Mix by hand until the meat mixture is smooth, but stop once the mixture looks even. (Overmixing can create a dense heavy texture.)

Ingredients

  • Beef - 1 lb (450 g)
  • Crushed crackers - 1/4 cup (30 g)
  • Egg - 1 large
  • Worcestershire sauce - 1 tablespoon
  • Milk - 1/4 cup (60 ml)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.
  2. Form into 4 equal patties.
  3. Cook on a preheated grill or skillet over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. Let rest for a few minutes before serving.

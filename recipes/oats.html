Basic Oatmeal

Recipe

Combine all ingredients into a small saucepan and turn heat to medium/high. Bring to a boil. Then, turn the heat down to low/medium and continually stir for around 3-5 minutes as the oatmeal cooks and thickens. Once oatmeal is at the desired consistency, remove from heat, and serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cup plain, unsweetened almond milk, any kind of milk works
  • 1 medium banana, mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • M1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a small saucepan and turn heat to medium/high.
  2. Bring to a boil. Then, turn the heat down to low/medium and continually stir for around 3-5 minutes as the oatmeal cooks and thickens.
  3. Once oatmeal is at the desired consistency, remove from heat, and serve immediately.
